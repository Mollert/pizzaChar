Hey where'd the Pizza go!
On this site you can either find the type of pizza you're hungry for or report on one you've had.
Click on either of those buttons (pizzas) below depending on if you are full or hungry.
When you click this pizza button you will be taken to a page where you can find a pizza to your liking from a local pizzeria, no national chains. If you haunt one of the national chains, give a local pizza a try. There is plenty of variety here in Charlotte. The choices are gathered from each one of your experiences and then categorized and listed so you can see the options each one offers. I do hope you find one that looks good to you!
If you click on this pizza button, you have just finished a great pizza from one of our local pizzerias and you want to share the type with all of us. You will be asked to check the multiple things that made the pizza one of your favorites. We will add it to the others and offer your experience to the next site visitor. Remember, only local chains and thanks for being a pizza informer!
